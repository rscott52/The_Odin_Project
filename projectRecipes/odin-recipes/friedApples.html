Fried Apples

If you want to experience an old school, delicious dessert after a hearty MidWestern meal, then you have found the right recipe!

Ingredients

  • 4 sliced Apples (chef's choice in type!)
  • 1 TBSP. Cinnamon
  • 1/4 Cup of Sugar
  • 2 TBSP. Butter
    • Ice Cream (chef's choice in type!)

Steps

  1. Toss apples in a bag/bowl with the cinnamon and sugar and mix together.
  2. Melt butter in pan.
  3. Once butter is melted, fry apple slices in the pan until soft.
    4. Serve with ice cream!
