Hot Coco

Who says you can't enjoy a nice cup of hot chocolate all year round!? Whether its a snowy, December night, or a sunny summer day, this is the hot coco recipe for you!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 Cup of Sugar
  • 1/4 Cup of Coco
  • 1/3 Cup of Hot Water
  • 4 Cups of Milk (Chef's Choice in Type)
    • 3/4 TSP. Vanilla
  • Marshmellows (Chef's choice in size!)

Steps

  1. Stir the sugar, coco, and hot water together in a pan to melt the sugar.
  2. Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly until it boils.
  3. Let it boil and stir for 2 minutes.
  4. Add in milk and continue to stir until it is close to a boil.
  5. Remove from the heat source and add in Vanilla.
  6. Whip the drink until it is frothy
  7. Pour into mugs, add marshmellows and enjoy!
