Oreo Truffles

Who doesn't love oreos?! These Oreo Truffles are a great alternative to your typical Oreos and Milk treat. They are a great snack!

Ingredients

  • 3 Rows of Oreos
  • 1 PKG. of Cream Cheese
  • Semisweet Chocolate

Steps

  1. Crunch up all oreos in processor and set aside some for later.
  2. Mix oreos and cream cheese together.
  3. Set mix in fridge.
    4. Melt chocolate
  4. Prepare oreo mix into balls
  5. Dip balls into chocolate.
  6. Sprinkle set aside crunched oreos on top.
  7. Place in fridge.
  8. Once hard, remove and enjoy!
